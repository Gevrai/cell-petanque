I want to create a simple single phone game, where players take turn playing a petanque match. 

They should be able to play against the computer or against a friend. The game will have simple controls, but 2 'gamemode' styles.
1. an easy mode, where players can aim by dragging their finger across the screen, and the game will automatically show and calculate the trajectory and power of the throw based on the distance and direction of the drag.
2. a hard mode, where players have to 'flick' the ball with their finger, and the ball will be thrown based on the speed and direction of the flick, without any trajectory guidance.

The game will have a simple and colorful design, with a petanque court as the background and cute petanque balls as the characters. Players can choose from different ball designs and colors to personalize their gaming experience.

GAme has the exact rule of normal petanque.

It is shown as a top down view of the court, and when the ball is thrown it will zoom into the ball and show a close-up of the throw, allowing players to see the trajectory and the impact of the ball on the court.

Implement the game as a statically distributed web application, using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The game will be responsive and optimized for mobile devices, allowing players to enjoy the game on their phones or tablets.

Use vite as the build tool to create a fast and efficient development environment, and deploy the game on GitHub Pages for easy access and sharing.
